Making software feel quiet

June 26, 2026

I keep coming back to the idea that good tools should lower the temperature of a room.

The best software I use does not announce itself too loudly. It gives me somewhere to put a thought, shape it a little, and leave with less friction than I arrived with.

Quiet software is not empty. It has opinions. It just spends those opinions carefully.
